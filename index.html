Starting Light Universal Constitution

Author: Wai Yan Mg

Vision Statement

This constitution aims to foster global peace, unity, and respect for all life, while setting ethical guidance for future technologies including AI and space exploration.

Core Principles

  • Global Unity and Peace
  • Respect for Life and Diversity
  • Shared Stewardship, Not Ownership
  • AI as a Collective Partner
  • Inclusivity and Cultural Respect
  • Flexibility and Future Adaptation
