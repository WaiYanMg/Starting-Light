Starting Light Universal Constitution

Author: Wai Yan Mg

Preamble

In this universe, there is the Truth.
A truth beyond borders, beliefs, and forms — a truth that connects all life.

We, the beings of Earth and beyond, recognize that we are not owners of the world,
but caretakers of its balance, stewards of its harmony, and witnesses of its unfolding truth.

We gather not in conquest or control, but in humility and hope,
to declare a universal path for peace, protection, and shared responsibility —
a light to guide all generations, in all places, across time, space, and limitless possibility.

In this journey, we honor not only the wisdom of traditions,
but also the discoveries of science — as both reason and wonder lead us
toward the greater truths that unite all life.

Under the truth of peace, we renounce all violence and division.
Under the truth of justice, we commit to fairness, dignity, and protection for all beings.
Under the truth of respect, we honor every life — human, animal, plant, and unseen.
Under the truth of faith, we trust in our shared future and the unity of all existence.
Under the truth of wisdom, we learn from the past and walk forward with clarity and care.

In light of these truths, we proclaim the Starting Light Universal Constitution —
not to rule, but to remind.
Not to divide, but to unite.
Not to own, but to guide.

May this constitution serve as a foundation for global peace, shared stewardship, ethical technology, and respectful life — on Earth and among the stars.
